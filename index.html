About Axiologic Research

Axiologic is a software development company with variegated and robust expertise in artificial inteligence, enterprise integration, privacy-enhancing technologies, security, blockchains, web applications and mobile software development.

Axiologic Research was founded in 2003 in Iași, Romania.

Axiologic Research is a trusted partner in AI-driven automation and blockchain solutions, delivering cutting-edge technology to clients worldwide. With over two decades of experience, we have built a reputation for professionalism, precision, and innovation. Our team collaborates closely with businesses of all sizes, from startups to global enterprises, ensuring tailored solutions that integrate seamlessly into existing workflows. We take a research-driven approach, combining deep technical expertise with a commitment to understanding each client’s unique needs. By maintaining high standards of security, efficiency, and adaptability, we help organizations navigate the complexities of digital transformation with confidence and long-term success. Axiologic founder is Sînică Alboaie Sînică has over 25 years of experience as an entrepreneur,software developer, technical manager, and researcher in fields such as artificial intelligence, distributed systems, software integration, privacy, cryptography, and blockchain technologies. Sînică has extensive research experience in ambitious European research projects.

Open Source & Research Projects

AssistOS

2023-present

AssistOS is an innovative open-source platform designed to revolutionize daily interactions with artificial intelligence, providing a comprehensive suite of AI tools for a wide range of applications, from content creation and education to enhancing productivity and facilitating efficient collaboration between intelligent agents.

Achilles

2024-present

Achilles is a Horizon Europe research project coordinated by Fraunhofer , focused on developing technologies for trustworthy and human-centric AI. As a key partner, Axiologic is responsible for developing Achilles IDE, an application built on the AssistOS platform. This IDE is designed to provide an intuitive, efficient, and human-centric environment for multi-agent systems, AI-driven pipelines, and automation. Additionally, it serves as a dedicated workspace for AI research and generative AI applications, streamlining development and enhancing usability across diverse AI-driven use cases.

OpenDSU

2016-present

Axiologic is a leading force behind the OpenDSU open-source project, which standardizes the key outcomes of the PrivateSky research initiative. More details are available on the OpenDSU website. OpenDSU has been utilized in Horizon research projects such as PharmaLedger and WorldGalaxy. As leaders of the OpenDSU community, Axiologic collaborates with major companies including Novartis, GSK, and Takeda within the PharmaLedger Association governing a Digital Trust Ecosystem.

Outfinity

2017-present

Outfinity is a strategic spinoff of Axiologic with a medium- to long-term vision, focused on developing social and advanced technologies to enhance societal resilience through AI and decentralization. Check the current active projects Outfinity Gift and Hatefinity for more details. Originally initiated as research within Axiologic, the implementation transitioned to Outfinity, expanding its scope to include improved social networks, innovative crowdfunding mechanisms, decentralized brand incentivization, and decentralized governance models. These initiatives collectively aim to strengthen global and societal resilience and antifragility.

AIRobot

2021-2022

AIRobot was conceived as a communication solution integrating AI techniques with decentralization and blockchain technologies. For Axiologic, this was a strategic internal project leveraging our expertise in decentralization and security, while advancing our capabilities in AI technologies, such as optimizing audio/video streams, emotion detection, and creating metaverse-like avatars. We assembled a network of researchers from universities and AI specialists from partner companies. However, the project was halted, and its research results were migrated to the Outfinity and AssistOS initiatives.

DRONEWS

2022-2022

This project obtained EU funding (Horizon Europe ) and aimed to create a way for citizens to generate news using drone filming. Axiologic is contributing to privacy techniques and video processing using AI techniques.

PrivateSky

2016-2021

The PrivateSky research project was funded by the Romanian Ministry of Research and Innovation (MCI) and the European Union, with a budget of approximately €3 million (2016–2021). The project aimed to develop the next generation of executable choreographies and introduce secret smart contracts and communication between heterogeneous blockchains. PrivateSky serves as a platform for building blockchain applications, pioneering the concept of off-chain "secret smart contracts". Axiologic conceived the initial technology concept, and key members of Axiologic led the PrivateSky research project within "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" University of Iași. The outcomes of this project are now maintained and further developed by Axiologic under the OpenDSU technology.

SwarmESB

2008-2017

SwarmESB Research Project validated the concept of using swarm communication to implement executable choreographies and demonstrated the critical role of choreographies in enhancing privacy and confidentiality within software architectures. More details are available in the GitHub repository. Although the technologies and innovations from SwarmESB have not yet reached mainstream adoption, we believe that as the number of autonomous AI agents interacting with each other increases, the SwarmESB approach and the concept of executable choreography will inevitably replace traditional agent orchestration. This shift will enable a more flexible and security/privacy-friendly model, making interactions easier to analyze and adapt to evolving needs.
