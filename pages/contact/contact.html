Axiologic is a specialized software development company, with especially strong and variegated expertise in integration, privacy-enhancing technologies, security, blockchain, online applications and mobile software development. Axiologic is involved in advanced research aimed at scaling blockchain-based systems, secure digital values, storage and at doing complex integration with smart contracts and executable choreographies. We research, design and implement in software the business processes and the integration of project components with the distributed ledgers. We are specialized in doing the implementation of the industry-specific Distributed Applications and of the smart contracts. In PrivateSky we contributed to the development of off-chain storages, choreographic smart contracts and various types of secret smart contracts. Axiologic is open to transfer technologies from the PrivateSky platform towards other more mainstream blockchains.
